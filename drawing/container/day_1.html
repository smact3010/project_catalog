Day 1 - Supplies, Layout, and Outline

Completed: 1/29/16

Idea: Try to take pictures at or before milestones (maybe steps).

Step 1
Collect supplies, workout layout details
Step 2 - Drawing layout
Divide paper into quarters, lengthwise, and locate the center of each section. Use harder pencils for outlining (H or 2H).
Step 3 - "Front" view outline
The "front" view will be the longest side of the container with the doors end to the right.
Based on the section center, draw a scaled 20'x8'6" rectangle as an an outline for the view. All other views will be based on this "front" view.
Step 4 - "Side" view outline
The "side" view will be the right end of the "front" view with the doors.
Based on the center and "front" view, draw a scaled 8'x8'6" rectangle as an outline for the view.
Step 5 - "Top" view outline
Based on the center and "front" view, draw a scaled 20'x8' rectangle as an outline for the view.
Milestone 1
Layout and outline complete
